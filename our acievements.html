Year Achievements
The Beginnings 1997 From a hostel room an idea of training ICT skills is conceptualised and started. From a few college mates the idea grows to become a class that can no longer fit in the hostel space. This idea is registered to operate as Zenith Technology (Zenith means at the very top) and the institution moves to Nairobi CBD.
The Birth of Zetech College 1999 Zenith Technology evolves and becomes Zetech College (Zenith + Technology) offering a wide range of Certificate and Diploma programs and later embarks on a 15-year strategic growth blueprint of becoming a leading university in Technology.
JKUAT Collaboration 2007 In pursuit of its vision of becoming a leading University, the college entered into a collaboration with Jomo Kenyatta University of Agriculture and Technology (JKUAT) to offer their degree programs as part of a mentorship program.
The Transition to Zetech University 2014 In line with its blueprint Zetech developed the Ruiru Campus and on 24th August 2014 received a Letter of Interim Authority (LIA) from the Commission for University Education giving it authority to operate as a University. Our flagship programs were Bachelor of Information Technology, Bachelor of Business Information Technology and Bachelor of Business Administration and Management. The University also launched a Sports Scholarship Fund which targets needy and talented students. This fund has seen many students enjoy a bespoke opportunity of pursuing their dream careers while still pursuing their talents in various sports.
The Inaugural Graduation and Program Expansion 2017 Graduated the first cohort of degree students on 10th November 2017, growth of degree program portfolio in the fields of Education, Hospitality, Procurement and Business and admission of first cohort of Government Sponsored Students in September 2017. The University launched the 2018-2022 Strategic Plan as a roadmap to attainment of charter
Award of the Charter 2022 On 2nd August 2022, Zetech was awarded the Charter to become a fully-fledged University by H.E. President Uhuru Kenyatta after having met all the required conditions by the Commission for University Education. Additionally, we opened doors to students at The Technology Park Mang'u Campus which serves as The Seat of the University.
Silver Jubilee 2023 The University launches Silver Jubilee to mark 25 Years of Scholarly Excellence, launches the 2022-2031 Strategic Plan, the Zetech University Foundation and the first PhD program. The Nairobi City Campus is accredited and launched providing convenience for working professionals and graduate students.
